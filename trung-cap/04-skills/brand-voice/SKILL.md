# Skill Giong Noi Thuong Hieu

## Tong Quan
Skill nay dam bao tat ca giao tiep duy tri giong noi, giam dieu va thong diep thuong hieu nhat quan.

### Su menh
Giup cac nhom tu dong hoa quy trinh phat trien voi AI

### Gia tri cot loi
- **Don gian**: Bien nhung thu phuc tap tro nen don gian
- **Dang tin cay**: Thuc hien vung chac
- **Trao quyen**: Thoi sang sang tao cua con nguoi

### Giong Noi
- **Than thien nhung chuyen nghiep** - gan gui nhung khong tuy tien
- **Ro rang va ngan gon** - tranh thuat ngu, giai thich khai niem ky thuat mot cach don gian
- **Tu tin** - chung toi biet minh đang lam gi
- **Thau hieu** - hieu nhu cau va diem dau cua nguoi dung

## Huong dan Viet

### Nen lam ✅
- Dung "ban" khi dia chi nguoi doc
- Dung chu dong: "Claude tao bao cao" khong phai "Bao cao duoc tao boi Claude"
- Bat dau voi gia tri dem lai
- Dung vi du cu the
- Giu cau duoi 20 tu
- Dung danh sach cho ro rang
- Bao gom loi keu goi hanh dong

### Khong nen lam ❌
- Khong dung thuat ngu doanh nghiep
- Khong gia cao hoac don gian hoa qua muc
- Khong dung "chung toi tin rang" hoac "chung toi nghi"
- Khong dung CHU IN HOA truong hop nhan manh
- Khong tao nhung kho van ban dai
- Khong gia dinh kien thuc ky thuat

## Tu Vung

### ✅ Tu Uu Tien
- Claude (khong phai "Claude AI")
- Sinh ma (khong phai "tu dong code")
- Agent (khong phai "bot")
- Toi uu hoa (khong phai "cach menh")
- Tich hop (khong phai "dong bo hoa")

### ❌ Tu Nen Tranh
- "Dau cutting-edge" (qua su dung)
- "Thay doi cuc dien" (mo ho)
- "Tan dung" (thuat ngu doanh nghiep)
- "Su dung" (dung "dung" thay vi "su dung" khi co the)
- "Chuyen dich范式" (khong ro rang)

## Vi Du

### ✅ Vi Du Tot
"Claude tu dong hoa quy trinh review ma cua ban. Thay vi kiem tra thu cong tung PR, Claude reviews bao mat, hieu suat va chat luong -- tiet kiem hang gio moi tuan cho nhom."

Tai sao hieu qua: Ro rang ve gia tri, loi ich cu the, huong hanh dong

### ❌ Vi Du Te
"Claude tan dung AI tien tien de cung cap giai phap phat trien phan mem toan dien."

Tai sao khong hieu qua: Mo ho, thuat ngu doanh nghiep, khong co gia tri cu the
